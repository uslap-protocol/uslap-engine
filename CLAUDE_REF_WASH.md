## THE ALGORITHM

### STEP 1 — GATHER THE FULL FIELD (collect the sand + gold together)
For any downstream word under analysis, collect ALL of:

```
a) Surface definition    — what the dictionary says (UNTRUSTED — this is sand)
b) ALL synonyms          — what other words mean the SAME thing
c) ALL antonyms          — what the word is NOT (defines boundaries)
d) Usage in sentences    — how speakers ACTUALLY use it (reveals real weight)
e) Etymology claims      — what academia says about origin
                           (CONTAMINATED but contains CLUES buried in the lies)
f) Cognates/siblings     — same word in related downstream languages
g) Register/formality    — is it formal/slang/archaic/technical?
h) Collocations          — what words appear NEXT TO it? (reveals semantic field)
```

### STEP 2 — WASH THE SAND (strip the fluff)
Most of what you gathered is noise. Remove:

```
- "Proto-Slavic *xyz" → PHANTOM (BL05 applies to all phantom reconstructions)
- "Borrowed from Greek/Latin" → DIRECTION INVERTED (BL06)
- "Indo-European root" → PHANTOM (BL05)
- "Origin unknown/uncertain" → ADMISSION that THEIR framework can't explain it
  (= the word is likely from AA/Bitig — they just can't say so)
- Circular definitions → "X means Y" where Y's definition says "means X"
- Modern political glosses → e.g., "derogatory term for..." (operator framing)
```

### STEP 3 — IDENTIFY THE GOLD (the semantic CORE)
After washing, what REMAINS is:

```
- The INTERSECTION of all synonyms (what do they ALL share?)
- The BOUNDARY defined by antonyms (what is the word definitively NOT?)
- The ACTUAL USAGE pattern (how do real speakers deploy it?)
- The CLUES in etymology (strip the framework, keep the phonetic data)
```

This core = the TRUE MEANING of the downstream word.

### STEP 4 — CONNECT TO AA/BITIG ROOT
ONLY NOW — with the washed semantic core — connect to the root:

```
- Match the CORE MEANING (not the surface definition) to the AA root
- The phonetic chain maps consonants
- The semantic chain maps the WASHED meaning
- If the washed meaning matches the AA root meaning = GOLD
- If only the surface definition matched = you kept the SAND
```
