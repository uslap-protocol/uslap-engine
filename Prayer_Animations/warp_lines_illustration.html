الخَطّ البَطْنِي السُّفْلِي / al-khaṭṭ al-baṭnī al-suflī / VENTRAL LOWER (BA016-017) — wraps from coccyx around pelvis → front of leg → toes
خَطّ جانِبِي / khaṭṭ jānibī / LATERAL LINE (BA019) — mastoid → lateral rib → lateral knee → lateral ankle
Psoas from L3-L4 — wraps forward through pelvis → meets stable conductor at فَخِذ / fakhidh / femur → merges with iliacus (from S1)
Ribs / أَضْلَاع / aḍlāʿ — horizontal rungs connecting dorsal rail to ventral rail (thorax only)
Negative space — مَفْصِل / mafṣil / joints — where warp rotates to allow movement
